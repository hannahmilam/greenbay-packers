GREEN BAY PACKERS
One of the OLDEST NFL franchises, established 1919.

The Green Bay Packers are the only non-profit NFL team and they have the most championships out of any of other NFL team. The Green Bay Packers have won FOUR superbowls. Their winning years were 2011 against the Pittsburg Steelers, 1997 against the New England Patriots, 1968 against the Las Vegas Raiders, and 1967 against the Kansas City Chiefs.
